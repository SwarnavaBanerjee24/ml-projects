Student Performance Predictor

Predicts the student's performance using the parameters given below

Gender

Gender can impact student performance through various factors, such as societal expectations, teacher biases, and access to resources.

Race or Ethnicity

Race or ethnicity can significantly impact student performance through factors such as socio-economic disparities, cultural differences, and systemic biases in the education system.

Parental Education

The parental level of education often impacts student performance, as parents with higher education levels are typically more equipped to provide academic support and create an enriching learning environment.

Lunch

Lunch plays a crucial role in student performance by providing essential nutrients that fuel the brain and body, enhancing concentration, memory, and overall cognitive function.

Test Preparation

Test preparation can significantly boost student performance by improving familiarity with test formats, reducing anxiety, and enhancing retention of key concepts.

Maths score

Math scores can heavily influence overall student performance as proficiency in mathematics often correlates with critical thinking skills, problem-solving abilities, and logical reasoning.

Maths score will be predicted using this web application

Reading Score

Reading scores significantly impact student performance across various subjects and academic domains.

Writing Score

A strong writing score is pivotal for student performance as it reflects proficiency in communication, critical thinking, and organizational skills.
